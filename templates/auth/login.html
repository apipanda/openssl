About Project

sslme.io is a free and open source ACME Protocol web client for generating trusted WEB PKI Certificates using the Let's Encrypt certificate authority. Let’s Encrypt is a free, automated, and open certificate authority (CA), run for the public’s benefit.

Free

Anyone who owns a domain name can use Let’s Encrypt to obtain a trusted certificate at zero cost.

Secure

Let’s Encrypt will serve as a platform for advancing TLS security best practices, both on the CA side and by helping site operators properly secure their servers.

Transperancy

The source code is available on GitHub, you can grab it and twist it to your heart’s content

Open

Let's Encrypt is OPEN. The automatic issuance and renewal protocol will be published as an open standard that others can adopt.

Cooperative

Much like the underlying Internet protocols themselves, Let’s Encrypt is a joint effort to benefit the community, beyond the control of any one organization.

Auto-renewal

Your SSL certificates are auto-renewed for you so you don't have to worry about contantly keeping your certificate.
